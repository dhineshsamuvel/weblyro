Common Problems Store Owners Face

Many businesses choose Shopify but struggle to generate consistent sales. Poor store setup, slow performance, confusing navigation, and lack of optimization often lead to abandoned carts and low conversion rates.

  • Using default themes without proper customization
  • Slow-loading pages that increase bounce rates
  • Confusing product structure and navigation
  • Product pages that fail to build trust or drive purchases
  • Checkout friction leading to abandoned carts
  • Poor mobile experience affecting sales
  • Result: low conversions and inconsistent revenue
